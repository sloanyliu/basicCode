How To Make Sushi

by Sloan Liu

Ingredients List

  • Some Semi-Dried Seaweed
  • Some Sticky Rice
  • Some Fresh Fish
    • Tuna
    • Salmon
    • Saba(mackrel)
    • Bluefin Tuna
    • Kampachi(greater amberjack)
    • Hamachi(yellowtail)
    • Shima-aji(stripped jack)
    • Engawa(halibut)
    • Iwashi(sardine)
    • Monkfish
    • Uni(sea urchin)
    • Albacore
    • Kurage(jellyfish)
  • Some Fresh Veg
    • Carrots
    • Cucumber
    • Pickled Radish
    • Seaweed Salad
    • Avocado

Directions

  1. Cut Fresh Fish Into Strips And/Or Pieces
  2. Cut Fresh Veg Into Strips
  3. Get Yourself A Bamboo Sushi Roller Sheet
    • Get You One On Amazon.com
  4. Lay A Generous And Even Layer Of Sticky Rice On The Entire Bamboo Roller Sheet
  5. Lay Pieces Of Semi-Dried Seaweed Over The Sticky Rice Layer
  6. Lay The Strips Of Fresh Fish And Fresh Veg On The Seaweed
  7. Roll The Roller Tight And Tuck It Under Until The Roller Fold In On Itself
  8. Take That Roll Out And Lay More Peices Of Fresh Fish And Fresh Veg On Top
  9. Drizzle With Some Sauce If You Got Some
  10. Cut It Up And Serve =)
